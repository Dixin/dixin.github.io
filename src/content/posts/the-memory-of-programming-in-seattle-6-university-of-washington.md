[University of Washington](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Washington), founded in 1861, is a public research university in [Seattle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle,_Washington). It is the largest university in the northwestern US and one of the oldest public universities on the west coast.

In 2008, it is placed 16th in the world's top universities, according to the so called [Academic Ranking of World Universities](http://www.arwu.org/rank2008/ARWU2008_A\(EN\).htm).
